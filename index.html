Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is a premier battle royale game developed and published by Krafton, tailored specifically for the Indian gaming community. Launched after the ban of its predecessor PUBG Mobile, BGMI has quickly become a favorite among gamers in India, offering a thrilling and immersive battle royale experience on mobile devices.

     

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) often runs various in-game events. These events can include themed challenges, tournaments, special item drops, or limited-time game modes. Events can vary widely and are usually announced through the game's official social media channels or within the game itself.
